Atlantis Advisor - registration info

Starting from version 2.3, Atlantis Advisor is shareware. That means, you may evaluate it as long as you wish, with limited access to some cool features. But sometimes you should pay for it and become registered user. Registration fee is 10$.

Why should you register

Because, as registered user, you:

  1. Encourage the author to further develop and improve software, making
    possible new releases;
  2. Gain the right to use ALL future versions;
  3. Gain access to the some advanced features;
  4. Get rid of all registration reminders;
  5. Access support and assistance via E-mail;
  6. Gain the right to request new features for future versions.

Limits of unregistered version

  • One game
  • Only "bin" avatar pack available; avatars can't be exported
  • One bookmark
  • Visual Battle Viewer and Battle Simulator available as trial only for one battle in Sample Game
  • No scripts

Registration procedure

You may register online by credit card. Please follow to website http://www.wredosoft.tk to gain more information.

Upon receipt of your registration fee you will receive a registration key which will correspond to your name.

Upon receipt of your registration key you should start Advisor with any or no game opened, choose "Register" from Help menu, then enter your name and the key when requested. This will activate the registered features of Advisor.

If you know any better way to send me money, please contact me by e-mail.
